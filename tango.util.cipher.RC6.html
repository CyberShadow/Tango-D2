Implementation of the RC6-32/20/b cipher designed by Ron Rivest et al. of RSA Security. It should be noted that this algorithm is very similar to RC5. Currently there are no plans to implement RC5, but should that change in the future, it may be wise to rewrite both RC5 and RC6 to use some kind of template or base class.
